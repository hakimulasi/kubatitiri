Dometi Pongo’s Professional Career

Dometi began working as a freelance spoken word artist and audio engineer after completing his bachelor’s degree in Economics at Target Corporation as an Analyst for the Business. After working at two different companies, he left the business sector to pursue journalism and broadcasting, but it wasn’t his goal to be a journalist. He began working on numerous broadcasting channels and shows, but his career took off when he began hosting MTV shows.

He has seen a surge in his career since 2016, when he received the Community leadership award and the best radio feature award in the same year. In 2017, he was named a finalist for the Peter Lisagor Award. In 2017, he was named one of the 40 Game hangers under 40. Following that, in 2019, he received the NAPA Ambassador Award. His most recent achievements include the Best COVID-19 Awareness Related Content, Best Live Event Coverage, and Best News and Media Video Series. He is now working on MTV’s True Life Crime.
