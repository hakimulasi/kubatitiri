Career

Tyler Oakley created his YouTube channel in 2007. In the beginning, he used to share videos for one of his friends. He has also co-hosted the weekly news update based on pop culture called “Top That!” in 2013.

In 2014, he was featured in the investigative report called “Generation Like” on Frontline.

Tyler Oakley released his first collection of personal essays called the ‘Binge’ in 2015. It was published by Simon & Schuster.

In 2016, he became the co-host of the show ‘The Tyler Oakley Show.’ The show aired weekly on the ellentube platform of Ellen DeGeneres.

He was named in the ‘30 under 30’ by Forbes in 2017. He competed in The Amazing Race season 28 along with Korey Kuhl and finished 3rd.
