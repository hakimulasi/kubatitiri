Is Don Lemon Married? Engaged Since April 2019

The CNN Newsroom host Don Lemon is in a same-sex relationship with his partner Tim Malone. They are a soon-to-be-married couple.

Tim proposed his love interest, Don, on 5 April 2019, when he was with his friends celebrating his birthday. Don's boyfriend popped the question with a two poodle mix where it was written, 'Daddy, Will you marry Papa?'

Tim said 'yes' to the marriage proposal of Don, which he opened on media through a picture that also showed the engagement rings of both the partners. He mentioned;

"He (Don) gave me a present on his birthday. How could I say no?"

It has already been over a year of their engagement; however, Don and Tim do not seem in haste to share the wedding vows. No signs of preparation and plans are made public by the soon-to-be-married couple.

We wish them good luck in starting their new family life.

How Much Does Don Lemon Make? His Net Worth And Salary

The CNN Tonight presenter, Don Lemon, sovereigns the net worth of $12 million and garners a salary of $4 million as well. Apart from that, he lives in his house in Sag Harbor, New York, which he bought for $3.1 million in 2016. He also owns a condo in New York’s Harlem neighborhood.

Don started his journalism career in his college years, where he was a news assistant at WNYW in New York City. Besides, he reported for WCAU in Philadelphia as a weekend anchor and anchor/investigative reporter for KTVI St. Louis.

Similarly, Don served as a reporter for NBC News' New York City operations, a correspondent for Today and NBC Nightly News, and an anchor on Weekend Today and MSNBC before he joined CNN in September 2006.

Don Lemon's Works At CNN

Since Don stepped into CNN, he has covered the I-35 bridge college in Minneapolis (2007), the death of Michael Jackson (2009), and the shooting violence at Sandy Hook Elementary (2012).
