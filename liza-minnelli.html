American Actress, Liza Minnelli Has Been Married And Divorced Four Times

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Liza Minnelli is a multi-talented celebrity who has worked in various fields and achieved success in different types of industries in the entertainment sector. She has worked in the show business for more than 70 years and in that time, she has amassed global fame. 

In addition to this, she is also one of the most widely respected artists in the world due to her theatre and Broadway background. Similarly, her drag-inspired style has made her a gay icon. 

Early Life Of Liza Minnelli

The beautiful actress, singer, a dancer was born Liza May Minnelli on March 12, 1946, in Los Angeles, California, United States. She is the daughter of Judy Garland and Vincente Minnelli. Both her parents are long deceased.

Liza also has three half-siblings. Brother, Joey Luft, and sister Lorna Luft, from Judy's marriage to Sid Luft. Similarly, she has a sister Christiane Nina Minnelli from Vincente's second marriage.

Regarding her education, she attended the High School of Performing Arts and later joined Chadwick School.

Liza Minnelli's Past Husbands

The Halston actress Minnelli was in a marital relationship with four men. Liza first got married to Peter Allen on March 3, 1967. But Allen and Minnelli's marriage did not last long. They divorced on July 24, 1974, after seven years.

Peter was Liza's mother's protege and their marriage relationship was quite complicated. Peter was a gay man who kept his sexuality a secret from Liza.

After that, Liza tied the knot with Jack Haley Jr when she got her first divorce. Due to unmentioned reasons, Jack and Liza also separated in April 1979.

Afterward, on December 4, 1979, Liza Minnelli married a sculptor, and stage manager, Mark Gero. Her marriage to Gero would be her longest-lasting one, as they were together for around 13 years.

Liza's fourth and final marriage was with producer, and TV personality, David Gest. They exchanged vows on March 16, 2002. However, Gest and Minnelli also separated. They finally divorced in April 2007, after four years of separation. 

Other than her marriages, Liza had relationships with Rock Brynner, Desi Arnaz, Jr, Peter Sellers, Randy Fenoli, and Martin Scorsese.

Does Liza Have Any Kids?

Liza Minnelli does not have any children. However, in the past, Minnelli did have a long-term pregnancy. Sadly,  the child did not survive, possibly due to a hiatal hernia. Nothing could be done to save her baby.

She has also had miscarriages in the past. She has talked about them in interviews.
