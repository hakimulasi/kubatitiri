Mate Pavi siblings: Meet sister Nadja Pavic

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, July 15, 2024

Mate Pavić. was born on 4 July 1993 in Split, Croatia. As of 2022, his age is 29 years old.

Mate Pavić has been active as a professional tennis player since 2011 and is still active today. Nonetheless, he was coached by John Farrington and Nađa Pavić.

Initially, Pavić earned a wild pass to the 2012 Zagreb Indoors doubles tournament after winning the Boys’ Doubles title at Wimbledon. Eventually, at the age of 18, he reached his first ATP doubles finals with Ivan Dodig.

Besides, Pavić aspired to play tennis in both singles and doubles, however, when doubles qualifying events were established in 2016, he was allowed to enter larger ATP doubles tournaments. Subsequently, he decided to concentrate on doubles. In addition, he and Laura Siegemund won the mixed doubles title in 2016.

Mate Pavić siblings: Meet sister Nadja Pavic

Nadja Pavic, Mate Pavic’s elder sister, was born on September 13, 1985. (36 years old). She is a Croatian tennis player with a career-high doubles ranking of No. 260.

Does Mate Pavić share the same parents with his sister?

The parents of Mate Pavić are Jakov, a tennis coach, and Snježana, a kindergarten teacher. When he was just a young boy, he watched his father coach his (Mate) sister.
