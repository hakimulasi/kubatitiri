FAQs about Ingrid Bolsø Berdal

Who is Ingrid Bolsø Berdal?

She is a Norwegian actress known for her roles in films like Cold Prey, Hercules, and the HBO series Westworld. Berdal is also a singer and film producer.

When and where was Ingrid born?

Ingrid was born on March 2, 1980, in Levanger, Norway.

What was her first leading role?

Her first leading role was in the 2006 horror film Cold Prey, for which she won the Amanda Award for Best Actress.

What is her most notable television role?

She is known for portraying the character Armistice in Westworld, a role she played from 2016 to 2018.

What other films has Ingrid appeared in?

Berdal has appeared in Hansel & Gretel: Witch Hunters, Chernobyl Diaries, and Hercules, among others.

Who manages Berdal?

Her management team includes one agent and two managers.

Where did she study acting?

Ingrid studied acting at the Oslo National Academy of Dramatic Arts for three years.

Has Ingrid won any awards for her acting?

Yes, she won the Amanda Award for Best Actress for her role in Cold Prey and was nominated for the same award for Cold Prey 2.

Has she hosted any award shows?

She has hosted the Norwegian Film Awards (Amanda Awards) twice, in 2010 and 2012.
