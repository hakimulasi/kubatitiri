Hal Smith Biography

Hal Smith is one of the most popular and richest TV Actor who was born on August 24, 1916 in Petoskey, Michigan, United States. Actor who played Otis Campbell on The Andy Griffith Show. He also voiced a number of memorable characters, such as Owl in Winnie The Pooh, Uncle Tex in The Flintstones and Flintheart Glomgold and Gyro Gearloose in Ducktales.

He and Jim Cummings occasionally rotated the voice of Winnie the Pooh in the late 1980s and early 1990s.

He was married to Louise C. Smith from 1936 until her death in 1992. He and Louise had a son named Terry.
