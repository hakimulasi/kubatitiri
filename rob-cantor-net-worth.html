Rob Cantor Full Overview and Wiki

Early Life and Education

Born on August 26 1983, in the tranquil town of Bloomfield Hills, Michigan, Rob Cantor was destined for the stage.

Growing up in a household where his father was an OB-GYN, Cantor was introduced to the discipline and dedication needed to succeed. Opting for the University of Michigan, he majored in molecular biology, a choice that speaks volumes about his intellect.

Yet, his passion for music, honed with a minor in nonprofessional music, eventually steered his career path towards the arts.

From Indie Bands to Viral Sensations

His journey into the spotlight began with the formation of Tally Hall in 2002, alongside bassist Zubin Sedghi and musician Andrew Horowitz.

FAQs about Rob Cantor

Who is Rob Cantor?

He is an American singer-songwriter, known for his work as a vocalist, guitarist, and co-writer for the indie rock band Tally Hall. He has also gained recognition for creating multiple viral videos.

What is Rob Cantor’s most popular work?

His most popular song is Shia LaBeouf, which has garnered significant attention and page views online.

He is also known for his work on the music video Rob Cantor: Shia LaBeouf Live and his contributions to the soundtrack of Pitch Perfect 3 and the short 29 Celebrity Impressions, 1 Original Song.

Has he won any awards?

Yes, he has won two Webby Awards for the video Rob Cantor: Shia LaBeouf Live, both in the Video – Comedy: Shortform category and the People’s Voice award in 2015.

Does he have any children?

He currently lives in Los Angeles, California with his wife, Allison Cantor née Kurtzer, and their two children, Milo and Leni.

What is Rob Cantor’s background?

He was born on August 26, 1983, in Bloomfield Hills, Michigan. He is Jewish and had his bar mitzvah at Temple Israel in West Bloomfield Township, Michigan.

Is Tally Hall still active?

Tally Hall became inactive after the release of their album Good & Evil and its subsequent tour, with members pursuing independent projects.

What other roles has he had in the entertainment industry?
