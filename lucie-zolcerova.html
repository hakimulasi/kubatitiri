Lucie Zolcerova

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, August 23, 2024

Lucie Zolcerova is the wife of American singer and songwriter Michael Grimm. Lucie's husband Michael is the winner of the fifth season of America's Got Talent.

Lucie got her GED from Fallbrook High School in 1997. She is also managing and booking her husband, Michael. She was born and raised in the Czech Republic 

Lucie and Michael's Marriage

Lucie's relationship with the singer started in 2007 before the singer went on America's Got Talent. Michael proposed to Lucie on September 16, 2010, during a taping of Ellen's, The Ellen Show. 

During the AGT's finals, Michael revealed that Lucie was the inspiration behind his performance of "When a Man Loves a Woman."

The Dream Proposal

On The Ellen Show, he told Ellen

"Once you find that good woman you hang on to her."

During the interview, Lucie was brought down from the audience by Ellen, and Michael got down on one knee and proposed to her. She of-course answered, YES!!. 

The love birds got married in Hawaii in 2012. The ceremony was held in front of 30 family members at the Makena Beach & Golf Resort.

Lucie and Michael's Net Worth

Lucie and her husband's net worth is estimated to be around $3 million. Her husband is the winner of the fifth season of America's Got Talent.

The competition aired between June 1 to September 15, 2010, with judges Sharon Osbourne, Piers Morgan, and Howie Mandel. Michael was alongside the runner up Jackie Evancho in the final. 

As the winner of the competition, Michael won $1 million. The large sum could be taken all at once or be paid at the rate of $25,000 per year for the next forty years.

Michael took the money and chose to spend it wisely. He built a new house for his grandparents, whose house was destroyed by Hurricane Katrina.

The AGT winner is signed to Epic Records. He released his self titled album in 2011. The album debuted at number 13 at the US Pop Music Charts and sold around 23,000 copies. He was previously signed with Mattikay Records before winning the AGT. These involvements are undoubtedly helpful to bump his net worth.

On December 3, 2010, Grimm performed in the 37th Annual Candlelight Concert. He appeared alongside Smokey Robinson. The concert raised over $1 million in charity.

Michael again returned to play in "The Champions" edition of America's Got Talent in 2020. But he was eliminated in the first round of the competition. The Golden Derby reported his exit as a shocker.

Business Endeavors

Lucie has also been busy all these years and is involved in multiple businesses. she was the owner of S.A.M.B.A a stage artist management and booking agency from 2012 to 2015. She is also the Merch Director of Mattikay LLC, which designs and creates all merchandise for her husband.

From 2014 Lucie is her husband's Booking Manager at Mattikay Touring Inc. Apart from that, she also owns Lucie's Apothecary, which servers hand-crafted remedies for all modern diseases.

She is also an author with her book "My Journey Home," published in 2012. The book tells the story of her journey from the Czech Republic to America.

Was Lucie In The Titanic?

Yes, Lucie Zolcerova was in the 1998 Academy award-winning movie Titanic. But she didn't have a significant role in the film. She had the part of a kissing woman aboard the Titanic.
