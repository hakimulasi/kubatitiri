Sexyy Red Stays 'Cozy' As She Rehearses For Drake Concert

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, September 12, 2024

Sexyy Red is the ultimate cozy girl when she’s getting ready to perform as her soundcheck outfit went viral over the weekend.

With the “Pound Town” rapper opening for Drake’s It’s All A Blur Tour, she was seen hitting soundcheck on Sunday (August 20) in a white nightgown and bonnet.
