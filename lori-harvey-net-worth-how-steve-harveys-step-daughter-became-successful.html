A glimpse into the successful and luxurious life of model and entrepreneur Lori Harvey. Find out Lori Harvey’s net worth.

27-year-old Lori Harvey is an American model, entrepreneur, and socialite. She has worked for prominent modeling agencies such as LA Models and Select Model Management. Lori started her modeling career at the age of three.

Lori was born in Memphis, Tennessee, but grew up in Atlanta, Georgia. As a child, she was a talented equestrian with aspirations to compete at the Olympic Games. Unfortunately, a riding accident left her with an injury that forced her to abandon her dream.

Lori Harvey’s Net Worth: How Did She Make Her Money?

Lori Harvey’s career has been nothing short of impressive. She first entered the modeling industry in 2015. In 2021, Lori Harvey expanded her entrepreneurial endeavors by launching her skincare line, SKN by LH. She also collaborated with Naked Wardrobe to create a clothing collection.
