Professional Career: Movies and TV Shows

Leila’s professional career started soon after she won the crown of Miss San Francisco in 1997. After that, the young model appeared in numerous commercials, including the one of Starburst. Likewise, she even appeared in music videos with Boyz II Men, The Isley Brothers, Q-Tip, and more.

Moreover, that same year, she made her television debut from the series Rescue 77. Till now, the American actress has appeared in more than dozens of movies and Television shows and has much success under her belt.
