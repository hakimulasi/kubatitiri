Mean Girls Candy Collection Inspired By Its Best Lines Is Here: Shop!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, July 6, 2024

Dylan’s Candy Bar is never going in our burn book!

In honor of the upcoming annual Mean Girls day (October 3, because “he asked me what day it was”), the shop’s luxury candy connoisseurs have put together a 15-piece collection of sweets and goodies honoring the film’s most memorable lines.

Revel in the genius employed here: “You Go Glen Coco” hot cocoa. “Is butter a carb?” popcorn-flavored jelly beans. There’s even an “I’m not a regular mom, I’m a cool mom” to-go wine cup.

You can also scoop up “On Wednesdays, we eat pink candy” T-shirts, cookies decorated to look like Burn Books, and Regina’s (Rachel McAdams) not-so-blessed Kalteen “nutrition bars” (which are now just made out of delicious crisp-filled peanut butter).
