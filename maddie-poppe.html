Maddie Poppe- Biography

Maddie Poppe (born December 5, 1997) is an American singer-songwriter, musician, and the season 16 winner of American Idol. She is a multi-instrumentalist who can play the guitar, piano, and ukulele. Before winning American Idol, Poppe released an EP titled Songs from the Basement. She also auditioned for The Voice in 2015.
