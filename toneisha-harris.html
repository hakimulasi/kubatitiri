Career Background

By the age of 12, Toneisha Harris was already singing in traveling gospel with her mother and aunt. The Voice star remained in the gospel group of 15 years before finally pursuing R&B, her actual music interest that was introduced by her late father.

Nevertheless, she couldn’t pursue a music career as her son was diagnosed with leukemia. Now he is recovered and Harris is back to give a second chance to her passion. She said in an interview,

“Music is a driving force for me … I like to say that music is like breathing to me. I am music; music is me. It is an intricate part of my life. It’s helped me through some difficult times.”
