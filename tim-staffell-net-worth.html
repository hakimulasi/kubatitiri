What is Tim Staffell's Net Worth?

Tim Staffell is an English musician, visual artist, and designer who has a net worth of $3 million. Tim Staffell was born in Ealing, London, England in February 1948. He is a bass guitarist who also plays harmonic and sings. Staffell was a part of the band Smile which later became known as Queen. He was the singer in the band the Railroaders and then formed the band 1984.

He became friends with Freddie Mercury at Ealing Art College. Staffell co-wrote the song "Doing All Right" which was featured on Queen's debut album. He later joined the bands Humpy Bong and Morgan.
