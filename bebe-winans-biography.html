BeBe Winans- Professional Career

– BeBe Winans started his career along with her sister Cece as a duo. Later, they appeared in Jim Bakker’s TV show, The PTL Club as a background vocalist. In 1984, under public demand, they even recorded their debut album, Lord Lift Us Up for the program.

– During the Mid-’80s, after leaving PTL, they signed with the Sparrow recording label and released albums like BeBe & CeCe, Heaven, and Different Lifestyles. 

– Moving forward, in 1995, he spilled from his sister and started pursuing his solo career. In 1996, he released his first single, All of Me.

-Signing with Atlantic Records he delivered his first single album in 1997. The same year, he released other singles like In Harm’s Way and Thank You under Atlantic.

– Leaving Atlantis, he signed with the recording label, Motown in 1999 and left after around four years to start his recording label, The Movement Group.

– In 2004, BeBe Winans made his first appearance in the movie with The Manchurian Candidate. In the movie, he played the character of an FBI agent name, Williams.

– As of now, he also hosts his radio program, The BeBe Winans Radio Show. Also, he has made his appearance as a judge in the real gospel signing show, Sunday Best.
