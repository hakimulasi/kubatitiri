Ward Thomas Tease Maddie &amp; Tae Collaboration

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 28, 2024

Record breaking UK country music act Ward Thomas have just wrapped the UK leg of their sold out ‘Cartwheels’ tour, but while they were in Manchester the girls found the time to meet up with US country music duo Maddie & Tae.

The girls have kept tight lipped about what they got up to when they met up with each other but Ward Thomas shared a snap of them with Maddie & Tae, with the caption: “@maddieandtae are in the U.K!! Keep your eyes and ears peeled for a little something”

@maddieandtae are in the U.K!! Keep your eyes and ears peeled for a little something ?????? pic.twitter.com/eNr5OT4bUD

— Ward Thomas (@wardthomasmusic) October 24, 2016

Judging by the snap and the tweet then it is looking very likely that the two country music duo’s have teamed up for a very special collaboration.

Maddie & Tae also shared the snap on their official Instagram account, with the caption: “We’ve been hanging out with the gals of @wardthomasmusic in the UK! Keep your eyes peeled for some exciting things coming up over the next month. #whoruntheworld” 

Make sure you keep your eyes peeled on Ward Thomas, and Maddie & Tae’s social media accounts for all the latest news regarding their possible collaboration.
