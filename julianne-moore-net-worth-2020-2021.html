Early life

The American actress was born as Julie Anna Smith on December 3, 1960, in Fayetteville, North Caroline, US. Her mother, Anne, was a social worker while her father, Peter Moore Smith, was an army officer at that time. So Julianne’s childhood was spent in moving city to city. Due to the fact, she also had to change her school many times.

As a child, Julianne travelled to different locations around the world with her parents. She completed her high school from Frankfurt American High School in Germany. Finally, her family moved to the US just after her schools. Here Julianne enrolled at Boston University and graduated with a degree in acting and theatre.

Personal Life

Before her marriage with Bart, she was the wife of John Gould Rubin. They were married for almost ten years, from 1986 to 1995.

Career

Julianne was clear with her dreams and career since childhood. After her schooling, she professionally learned every nuance of acting and then, moved to New York City to make her career in Hollywood. Success didn’t come to her easily. In the initial days, she even had to work as a part-time waitress for a brief period.

However, soon, she managed to land her first role on the big screen. She bagged a role as Frannie Hughes on the popular soap opera ‘As the world turns.’ She impressed the people with her remarkable acting skills which led her to make her film debut in 1990 with the film ‘Tales of the Darkside.’ However, she was yet to taste success.

1993 happened to be a great year for Julianne. That year, she appeared in four movies, and they all were commercially successful. The following year, she established herself as a skilled actress with the film ‘Safe.’ Afterwards, she went on giving hits after hits and appeared in unnumbered of films and TV shows.

In 2014, her career took another leaf of success when she took the world by storm by her film ‘Still Alice’. She has also written a few books and had fair success with them.

Awards & achievements
